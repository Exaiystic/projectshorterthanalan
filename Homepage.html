About Us

After last racing in 2001, Newell and Wright Motorsport is back on the track after 18 years of absence! In 2018 we returned with a brand new custom built MAN racing truck, made from scratch with the help and expertise of Stefan Honens Racing Services and MAN. Our time away from the track hasn’t hurt our performance though! We’ve been coming 1st and 3rd in races in 2018, and that was with just four races under our belts! All the family is involved with this team, and everyone is looking forward to returning to the track in 2019! Who knows, we might even win the championship!

Driver

John Newell, our experienced driver, is the current racing driver for NWT Motorsport. John has been racing for us for seven years, but has been racing for 20 years. He has already taken 1st and 3rd place this year after 18 years away from the track!
